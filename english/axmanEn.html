Axmann Pub

Pub in the center of Osijek with an attractive interior
is a perfect place for morning coffee, drinks after work
and a fun place for going out and enjoying live gigs.
Axmann pub is named after the architect
and an urban planner Viktor Axmann who is one of
the most deserving people for today's look of the city of Osijek.
His most significant works are exhibited on our walls,
and you can see some of them through our windows.
